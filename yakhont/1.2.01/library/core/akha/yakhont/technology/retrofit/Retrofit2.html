    public class Retrofit2<T,D>
extends BaseRetrofit<T,retrofit2.Retrofit.Builder,retrofit2.Callback<D>,D>
    The component to work with Retrofit 2.x APIs.

    Every loader should have unique Retrofit2 object; don't share it with other loaders.

    See Also:
