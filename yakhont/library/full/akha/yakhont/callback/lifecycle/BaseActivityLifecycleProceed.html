  • Direct Known Subclasses:
    BaseActivityLifecycleProceed.ActivityLifecycleProceed

    public abstract class BaseActivityLifecycleProceed
extends BaseCallbacks.BaseLifecycleProceed
    Extends the BaseCallbacks.BaseLifecycleProceed class to provide the Activity lifecycle support. For the moment supported lifecycle is similar to the Application.ActivityLifecycleCallbacks but will likely expand in future releases.
